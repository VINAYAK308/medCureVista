Dengue Management Guide

Lifestyle

  • Rest: Prioritize sleep and avoid strenuous activities.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of fluids (water, coconut water) to prevent dehydration; avoid caffeine and alcohol.
  • Nutrition: Eat light, nutritious meals focusing on fruits and vegetables; avoid heavy foods.
  • Fever Management: Monitor temperature and use acetaminophen for fever relief as directed.
  • Symptom Relief: Use prescribed medications for pain and cold compresses for comfort.
  • Hygiene: Wash hands frequently and use mosquito repellent to prevent bites.
  • Environment: Stay cool and eliminate standing water around the home to reduce mosquito breeding.
  • Physical Activity: Limit exertion and focus on rest until recovery.
  • Mental Well-Being: Stay positive and engage in relaxing activities.
  • Follow-Up Care: Attend follow-up appointments and report any worsening symptoms.

This guide helps manage dengue effectively. Always consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
