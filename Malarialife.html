Malaria Management Guide

Lifestyle

  • Rest: Prioritize sleep and take naps as needed.
  • Hydration: Drink plenty of fluids (water, herbal teas) and avoid caffeine and alcohol.
  • Nutrition: Eat light, nutritious meals in small, frequent portions.
  • Fever Management: Monitor temperature and use antipyretics like acetaminophen as directed.
  • Hygiene: Wash hands frequently and use mosquito nets/repellents.
  • Medication: Take antimalarial medications as prescribed and complete the full course.
  • Symptom Relief: Manage pain with prescribed medications and seek help if symptoms worsen.
  • Environment: Stay in a cool, ventilated space and reduce mosquito exposure.
  • Activity: Limit physical exertion and engage in light activities as energy allows.
  • Mental Well-Being: Stay positive and seek support from family and friends.
  • Follow-Up: Attend follow-up appointments and report any new symptoms.

This guide helps manage symptoms and supports recovery from malaria. Always consult a healthcare provider for personalized advice.
