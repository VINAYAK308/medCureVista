Dengue Management Guide

Medicine

For dengue fever, the treatment primarily focuses on symptom relief and managing complications, as there is no specific antiviral treatment. Common medications include:

  • Acetaminophen (Paracetamol): Used to reduce fever and relieve pain. It's the preferred option as it is safer than non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) for dengue patients.
  • Hydration Solutions: Oral rehydration salts (ORS) or intravenous fluids may be necessary to prevent dehydration, especially in severe cases.
  • Avoiding NSAIDs: Medications like ibuprofen and aspirin are generally avoided, as they can increase the risk of bleeding.

Always consult a healthcare professional for proper diagnosis and treatment recommendations, especially in cases of severe dengue.
