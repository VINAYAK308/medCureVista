Diabetes Management Guide

Lifestyle

  • Healthy Eating: Focus on whole grains, lean proteins, healthy fats, fruits, and vegetables; monitor carbohydrate intake.
  • Regular Physical Activity: Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate exercise weekly, including strength training twice a week.
  • Weight Management: Maintain a healthy weight; set realistic weight loss goals if overweight.
  • Blood Sugar Monitoring: Regularly check blood glucose levels and keep a log.
  • Medication Adherence: Take medications as prescribed and discuss any side effects with your healthcare provider.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water; limit sugary drinks.
  • Limit Alcohol: Consume alcohol in moderation and consult your healthcare provider.
  • Manage Stress: Practice stress-reduction techniques like yoga and ensure adequate sleep (7-9 hours).
  • Regular Check-Ups: Schedule medical visits for monitoring and screenings.
  • Education and Support: Stay informed about diabetes and seek support from groups or healthcare professionals.
